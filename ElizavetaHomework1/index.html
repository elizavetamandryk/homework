The most famous dinosaurs of the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods

The size of the Tyrannosaurus rex is impressive. 12 meters long, 4 meters hip height, and about 7 tons of weight. The huge skull, seated with dozens of long teeth, could be 1.5-2 meters long and completely fit a person. The teeth of a tyrannosaurus rex are absolute champions among the teeth of all land predators.
Velox - fast and raptor - hunter - a genus of carnivorous bipedal dinosaurs from the family of dromaeosaurids, subfamily Velociraptorina. The type species is Velociraptor mongoliensis. He lived at the end of the Cretaceous period, about 75-71 million years ago. Its fossil remains were first discovered in Mongolia.
Adult individuals of different species of Triceratops reached from 6.1 to 7.6 meters in length, the length of the skull was 2.4 meters, the height varied from 2.4 to 3 meters, body weight - from 7.3 to 10.9 tons. Gregory Paul estimated the animal to be 8 meters long and weigh 9 tons.
